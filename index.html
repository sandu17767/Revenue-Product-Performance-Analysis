Black Friday Signal
November 2017 hit R$1,153,364 — 53% above October. Q4 alone generated 42% of full-year revenue. One month can make or break the annual target.
2018 Floor Stability
Every 2018 month stayed above R$966K. Unlike 2017's volatile ramp-up, 2018 shows a maturing business with predictable monthly floors.
AOV Flat — Untapped Upside
AOV held at R$128–R$152 across 2 years. A R$10 AOV increase across 96K orders = R$965K additional revenue from existing customers.
